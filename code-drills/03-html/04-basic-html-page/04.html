How to make a peanutbutter & jelly sandwich

  1. Get peanutbutter, jam, bread, and a butter knife.
  2. Layer peanutbutter and jam on the face of 2 pieces of bread
  3. Layer on piece of covered bread ontop of the other piece
  4. Enjoy!
